About CyberShield

Your AI-powered shield against online threats

What We Detect

🧠

Cyberbullying

Our AI detects toxic, abusive, and threatening language in messages and social media posts in real time.

💸

Scam Messages

We identify common scam patterns like fake prizes, urgent payment requests, and phishing attempts using AI and keyword analysis.

🔗

Malicious Links

Every URL is checked against Google Safe Browsing database and scanned for suspicious patterns like fake brand domains and IP-based links.

🚨

Repeat Harassment

CyberShield tracks repeated flagged messages from the same sender and triggers an automatic repeat harasser warning after 3 flags.

How To Protect Yourself

🛑 Cyberbullying

  • Never respond to abusive messages
  • Block the sender immediately
  • Screenshot and report to the platform
  • Talk to a trusted adult or authority

⚠️ Scam Messages

  • Never share OTP or bank details
  • Ignore "you've won" messages
  • Verify sender identity independently
  • Never send money to strangers online

🔗 Suspicious Links

  • Never click links from unknown senders
  • Check URL spelling before clicking
  • Avoid shortened URLs from strangers
  • Use CyberShield to verify any link

Where To Report

📱 Instagram

Go to the profile → Three dots → Report → It's inappropriate

💬 WhatsApp

Open chat → Three dots → Report → Block contact

🐦 Twitter / X

Click three dots on tweet → Report tweet → Select reason

👤 Facebook

Click three dots on post → Find support or report → Select category

🚔 Cybercrime India

Report at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930 national helpline

📧 Email Scams

Forward phishing emails to report@phishing.gov.in
